Target Audience

Description

The target audience for this website varries. We will mainly be focussing on people who come to the site looking for information on businesses and events in the area, and businesses that wish to join the chamber of commerce. This site will allow the two to interact and ultimately further our goal to make our city better.

Personas

Persona: Local Boutique Owner
Photo:
Fictional name: Angela Mathews
Job title: Owner/Manager
Demographics:
  • 35 years old
  • Married
  • Mother of two children
  • Has a B.S. in business management
Goals and tasks: She is a very hard worker with great leadership skills. She leads by example and dedicates her free time to furthering the success of her business. Because her boutique is not a chain, her main goal is to get the word out about her business.
Site access: She has a business computer that she uses throughout the day and mainly uses her phone at home.
Persona: New Home Owner
Photo:
Fictional name: Tiffany Jones
Job title: Registered Nurse
Demographics:
  • 36 years old
  • Single
  • Has a B.S. in nursing
Goals: Tiffany is a new homeowner here in town. This was her first time buying a house and is excited to live in the area. Since she is new to the area, she is looking for information on places to go and things to do here in town.
Site access: She mainly uses her tablet to surf the web and her phone for apps.

Scenarios

  • A person is looking for a new bank or credit union to use. They would like something close because they deposit checks in person. This site will have information on different financial institutions in the city.
  • A person is looking for a way to promote their new business. This site will give that business owner information on how to join the chamber of commerce and the benefits of doing sol.
  • A person is wanting to start playing a recreational sport. They can find info from businesses, such as gymns, that have sports courts to practice on. They will also find information on sports leagues created by the city.
  • A person is looking to attend local events to make new friends. This site will provide information on upcoming events held by the city and local businesses.
  • A person who has just lost their job is looking for ways to save some money. Many businesses will post coupons and discounts on the site for customers to utilize.
