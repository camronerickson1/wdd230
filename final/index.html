Provo City Center

The Provo City Center temple is a beautiful house of worship located in the heart of Utah Valley. This temple offers clothing rental and a great visitors center. A distribution center, local shopping, and restaurants are located nearby for all your needs.

This temple was dedicated on March 20, 2016. You can read the dedicatory prayer here.
